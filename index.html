Travelling First

Our Story

Hello, our names are Adrien and Camille, 23 years old and we come from La Rochelle in the south west of France. Since 2016, after our studies, we travel as often as possible around the world, only a few weeks and sometimes we travel longer up to a few months.

We had already started to travel before our meeting, Adrien had been in Berlin and for my part I had been in Venice and in Greece. Then together we did Amsterdam, London, the Pyrenees (France) and a long 9 month trip around Australia!. Now back in France we immerse ourselves in studies to prepare our future, but we hope to leave soon enough on a trip!

We always take a camera with us , a drone and everything we need to capture and share these incredible moments. Traveling around the world is our biggest dream and sharing this dream through images is a passion that we developed during our previous trips.

Embark on our adventure, follow us and discover our current or already completed trips.
